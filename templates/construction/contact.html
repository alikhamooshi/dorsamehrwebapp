Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our services

What types of projects do you handle?

We specialize in residential, commercial, and industrial construction projects, including new builds, renovations, and infrastructure development.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary depending on size and complexity. Small renovations might take 2-4 weeks, while large commercial projects can take 6-18 months.

Do you provide free consultations?

Yes, we offer free initial consultations to discuss your project requirements and provide preliminary estimates.

What areas do you serve?

We primarily serve Tehran and surrounding areas, but we also take on projects throughout Iran depending on the scope and requirements.

Do you offer warranty on your work?

Yes, we provide comprehensive warranties on all our work. The warranty period varies depending on the type of project and materials used.

How do I get a quote for my project?

You can contact us through phone, email, or by filling out the contact form above. We'll schedule a consultation to discuss your project in detail.
